ABOUT US

J' MUGS BISTRO was established on 2020 and is located in the small town of Candoni, Negros Occidental. We are happy to serve you delicious and affordable meals and refreshments while enjoying the calm nature vibe of our restaurant. This is a great restaurant to visit whenever you somehow got lost and ended up here in our town.

OUR MENU

Our menu consists of several meals and refreshments that are delicious and affordable. We have a good amount of selection of cuisine that can cater people of all ages. There is always a food here that you will like. Oh, and did I mention that our meals here are affordable?

OUR SERVICES

Aside from eating here in our restaurant, we also provide catering services for all kinds of events. We provide catering services for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries and business meetings. And did you see those birthday surprise car trunk decorations? Yes, we do that too!
